My Hobbies

Church

My relationship with Jesus is more than a hobby, this is the core of who I am . If I can not get to anything else in my day, it is fine as long as I have spent time with Jesus.

Running

In June 2025, I started my running journey. It started off with me just wanting to lose more weight. Very quickly, I started enjoying it and have adopted it into my being. I am currently training for a Half- Marathon.

Reading
